# LaunchPulse: Global Spaceflight Trends Dashboard

LaunchPulse is an interactive Tableau dashboard that explores global rocket launch activity from 1957–2020.

The project analyzes more than 4,300 space missions to uncover trends in launch activity, mission reliability, and geographic launch distribution.

---

## Project Overview

Space exploration has evolved dramatically over the last six decades. LaunchPulse visualizes how global spaceflight activity has changed across time, organizations, and locations.

The dashboard highlights:

- global launch trends across decades
- leading launch organizations
- mission success vs failure patterns
- geographic launch activity

---

## Dashboard Features

• **Launch Trends Over Time**  
Shows how global launch activity evolved from the early space race to the modern commercial era.

• **Top Launch Organizations**  
Identifies organizations responsible for the highest number of launches.

• **Mission Success Analysis**  
Examines reliability across thousands of recorded missions.

• **Global Launch Locations**  
Geospatial visualization of launch activity worldwide.

## Key Insights

- Launch activity increased significantly during the Cold War era.
- A small number of organizations dominate global launch activity.
- Mission success rates are high across most launch providers.
- Launch sites are geographically concentrated in a few regions including the United States, Russia, and China.
